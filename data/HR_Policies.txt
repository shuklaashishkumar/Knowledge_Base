Article Title: HR Policies
Category: Human Resources
Last Updated: August 26, 2025

---------------------------------
Overview
---------------------------------
This article provides an overview of the HR policies at Learning Thoughts. These policies are designed to ensure a professional, fair, and safe work environment for all employees.

---------------------------------
1. Code of Conduct
---------------------------------
- All employees must maintain professionalism, integrity, and respect in the workplace.
- Harassment, discrimination, or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.
- Confidentiality of company information and customer data must always be upheld.

---------------------------------
2. Attendance & Leave Policy
---------------------------------
- Employees are expected to adhere to their assigned work schedules.
- Leave types include: Casual Leave, Sick Leave, and Earned Leave.
- Prior approval from reporting managers is required for planned leaves.
- In case of emergencies, employees must inform HR as soon as possible.

---------------------------------
3. Working Hours & Remote Work
---------------------------------
- Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday to Friday.
- Remote work is subject to manager approval and performance requirements.
- Employees must ensure availability during core business hours.

---------------------------------
4. Compensation & Benefits
---------------------------------
- Salaries are credited on the last working day of each month.
- Employees are entitled to statutory benefits such as Provident Fund, ESIC, and Gratuity (as applicable).
- Additional perks may include health insurance, training programs, and performance-based incentives.

---------------------------------
5. Performance & Appraisals
---------------------------------
- Performance reviews are conducted annually.
- Appraisals are based on Key Performance Indicators (KPIs), team contributions, and overall behavior.
- Constructive feedback will be shared with employees to support career growth.

---------------------------------
6. Workplace Safety & Compliance
---------------------------------
- All employees must comply with workplace safety regulations.
- Any incidents, hazards, or safety concerns must be reported to HR immediately.
- The company maintains compliance with local labor laws and regulations.

---------------------------------
7. Disciplinary Actions
---------------------------------
- Violation of HR policies may result in warnings, suspension, or termination.
- The severity of action depends on the nature of the violation.

---------------------------------
Troubleshooting / FAQs
---------------------------------
Q1: How do I apply for leave?
A1: Submit your leave request in the HR portal or email HR with prior approval from your manager.

Q2: Who do I contact for payroll issues?
A2: Reach out to the HR Payroll Officer at hr@learningthoughts.com.

Q3: Can I work from home permanently?
A3: Permanent remote work is subject to management approval and business needs.
